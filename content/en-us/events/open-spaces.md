---
title: Open Space
---

Open Space is a participant-driven gathering that takes place alongside the main conference schedule. Unlike pre-arranged talks, Open Space sessions are created and led on the spot by attendees. You can share a topic of interest (not limited to technical subjects), start a discussion, have casual chats, or simply hang out and connect with others—the format and content are entirely up to you.

If you’ve attended PyCon Taiwan or other conferences before, you may be familiar with BoF (Birds of a Feather) sessions. We use the term “Open Space” to make the concept more intuitive, especially for first-time attendees. It’s an open, flexible time for free-form conversations and spontaneous connections.

No preparation needed—just bring your ideas and enthusiasm!Anyone can host or join an Open Space session. Come with curiosity, invite a friend, and help spark fun and meaningful conversations throughout the event.
