---
title: Recording Release
---

Since one of the PyCon motivations is to help Python education and advocate the use of Python. PyCon Taiwan Committee will record, edit, and release the audio and video of the speaker’s presentation to online video sites such as YouTube and [pyvideo.org](https://pyvideo.org/) by default.

::prose-two-col{title="Sponsors' Logos"}
Thanks to our sponsors, we could make PyCon Taiwan one of the greatest events. We will embed sponsors' logos in the presentation videos that play during and after the conference. If you don’t want your talk being uploaded after the conference, make sure you choose “No” to the “Recording Release” option during the proposal submission.
::
